// HANDRECEIPT — COMPANY-LEVEL ERP

ORIGIN

This was the first product I built while separating from the Army. It came from daily frustration: property accountability at the company level and below was still paper-based, with DA Form 2062s and sub-hand receipts creating blind spots between the company property book and individual soldiers. I set out to digitize that layer.

The Problem (Company & Below)

  • Sub-hand receipts tracked on paper (DA 2062) with no reliable digital trail
  • Gaps between GCSS-Army property book and individual responsibility
  • Time-consuming audits; difficult chain-of-custody and change history

What I Built

// Core capabilities
• OCR/LLM digitization of DA Form 2062 (extract items & serials)
• Digital twin of each asset with sub-hand-receipt linkage
• Immutable audit log using ImmuDB for chain-of-custody
• Web + iOS + Android apps for line-level use
• QR code item lookup & quick transfer flows
Tech: Go/Gin, PostgreSQL, React, SwiftUI, Kotlin/Compose, ImmuDB, LLM APIs
Workflow (Paper → Digital)
Scan 2062 → OCR + LLM Parse → Validate Items/Serials → Create Digital Sub-HR → Ledger Write (ImmuDB) → QR Lookup / Transfer
  • Human-in-the-loop review on low-confidence parses
  • Every issuance/turn-in writes a signed, append-only record
Status & Lessons
  • Built post-Army as a practical fix for sub-hand-receipt visibility
  • Focused on accuracy, auditability, and ease for supply sergeants
  • Kept claims conservative: this slide intentionally removes ROI/adoption numbers
Why It Matters for Apex
  • Pattern: paper→digital ingestion + immutable lineage + quick field UX
  • Direct analog to spacecraft component custody and tool-cradle tracking
  • Experience designing an ERP-like system from zero with strong governance
