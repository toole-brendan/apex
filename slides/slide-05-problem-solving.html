DEPLOYMENT CRISIS RESOLUTION
SITUATION
48 hours before deployment inspection, vehicle systems showing non-mission capable status. Would cancel entire company deployment to Germany.
ROOT CAUSE
GCSS maintenance and supply modules not syncing - data integrity issue between subsystems creating false readiness reporting.
ACTIONS TAKEN
1. Physical verification of all 42 vehicles 2. Manual bridge between maintenance and supply modules 3. Direct coordination with DLA for priority parts 4. Lateral transfer agreements with sister units 5. 24-hour operations center established
RESULT
100% vehicle serviceability achieved. Company deployed on schedule. Permanent fix implemented across brigade.
