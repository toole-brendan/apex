// GCSS-ARMY ERP SYSTEM

SAP-based unit-level ERP used to manage supply, maintenance, and property accountability; daily system of record for a line company
Requisitions • Property Book • Maintenance • Distribution • EDI

HOW I USED IT (OFFICER BATTLE RHYTHM)

1) Requisition Management & Supply Flow (~60%)

Review overnight status; triage rejections, correct, resubmit; upgrade priorities
Create/modify requests; validate NSNs, quantities, fund codes, priorities
Track backorders & ETAs; chase delayed shipments; mass uploads for field

2) Property Book & Accountability (~25%)

Generate primary/sub hand receipts; monthly sensitive item inventories
Process adjustments, lateral transfers, turn-ins; maintain audit trails
Parallel issue trackers to bridge bulk system to individual distribution

3) Readiness & Maintenance Support

Monitor deadlined items; link parts to work orders; escalate aged lines
Research alternates; coordinate lateral transfers; stage services

Reports & Controls I Ran

Open requisitions/due-ins (company roll-up; flag aged lines)
Due-outs for maintenance (parts blocking FMC by work order)
Property by LIN with Serial/UII (inventories & change-of-command)
Sensitive item roster & shortage summaries
